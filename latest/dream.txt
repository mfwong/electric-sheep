a stop-motion animated short film noir set in a miniaturized dollhouse world, where a sharp-dressed hamster detective and his trusty sidekick, a wise-cracking talking spider, solved a murder mystery amidst the tight-knit community of anthropomorphic furniture and appliances, all while battling against the sinister forces of an evil toaster in a thrilling action sequence that culminated in a heartwarming dance party to celebrate the victory of justice.
